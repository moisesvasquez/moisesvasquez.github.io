Services

Dr. Peter A. Barone, Esq. is a Legal and Use of Force Expert and a Criminologist. An experienced courtroom witness who has testified in and been part of thousands of legal proceedings including depositions, hearings, and trials. He conducts seminars for Law Enforcement Personnel, Attorneys and Physicians approved for CLE and CME credits. He has a Ph.D. in Public Safety and Criminal Justice; an LL.M degree (post-doctorate) in International Law; a J.D. in Law; an M.S.M. in Management; a B.S. in Human Services Criminal Justice, and an A.S, a degree in Law Enforcement. He has published a book; chapters in books and several articles and has been invited and presented at several National Conferences. His major area of research is to examine 6,000 Officer Involved Shootings from police agencies in all 50 states.
